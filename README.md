# Lost Home

**A 2D RPG developed in Unity.**

---

## **The Story**

You are a lone mercenary, a drifter of the outer systems. Your only home is your ship—until a catastrophic jump failure sends it crashing into the depths of a hostile, sprawling cyber-city.

You survive, but your ship is crippled. Its essential power core has been stolen, making escape impossible. To get off this planet, you must navigate the ruthless, gang-ruled streets, fight your way through, and take back what is yours.

This is no longer a mission. It's a fight for survival.

---

## **Gameplay**

*   **Explore** three distinct districts of a vast cyberpunk metropolis.
*   **Fight** against two types of enemies and face a challenging final boss.
*   Experience a concise story of grit and resilience in a merciless urban sprawl.
